I specialize in developing innovative solutions at the intersection of data science, geoscience, and technology. With expertise in advanced machine learning techniques, geospatial data modelling, and predictive analytics, I create scalable, data-driven solutions that address challenges in environmental resilience, climate adaptation, and hazard risk assessment. My work bridges research and infrastructure, delivering actionable insights and transforming complex datasets into impactful outcomes. I'm motivated to tackle real-world problems by harnessing the power of technology and data science.
